The future of food distribution and logistics overall has been rapidly evolving, integrating new types of technology like Blockchain as a means to enable a secure, transparent, and decentralized means for tracking the entire journey of a product.

Blockchain is extremely useful when you need to track every movement within the food supply chain, and serves as the best alternative for tracking products, as each transaction is time-stamped and verified by the network, making it immutable and tamper-proof.

Within the food industry, Blockchain can be used to track the origin of ingredients, monitor the conditions in which they were grown or processed, and record the journey of the food product from the farm to the consumer. This can help to prevent fraud, improve food safety, and reduce waste.

Since logistical operations become ever more complex, it is vital for companies to have the capability of being able to identify the source of every transaction and/or possible contamination of the product within the supply chain.

The use of blockchain technology in food traceability can be broken down into three main stages: data collection, data management, and data sharing. At the data collection stage, sensors and other devices are used to gather information about the food products, such as their origin, production process, and transportation route.

This data is then stored on the blockchain, where it is accessible to all participants in the supply chain. And at that point we apply data processing and analysis in order to identify outliers, or issues within the supply chain, improving food safety.

This project does not handle an specific objective, as the project's only aim is to simulate a supply chain through the generation of blockchain. (Let's take into account that Blockchain technology relies on a network of computers to process and validate transactions.)

NOTE: Within this project we won't consider any data quality issues like anomalies or missing data as all data is generated specifically for this project, additionally every exercise with Blockchain will only be handled locally.
